Snooker

Snooker is a game involving balls on a large table. The players use long sticks called cues to hit a white ball, and score points by knocking coloured balls into the pockets at the sides of the table.



Rules

The snooker rules are simple. It includes 21 object balls (15 red and 6 colored balls) and one cue ball. Players have to pot a red ball first, followed by a colored ball, a red ball, a colored ball, and so on. The red-colored sequence continues until all of the balls have been pocketed.
